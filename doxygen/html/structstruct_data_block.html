◆ executeQueryModeWhereClause

char* executeQueryModeWhereClause

Where clause statement.

It is used only in "Execute Query" mode. Before executing query of the data block, the contents of this variable are added to the where clause. After each executing, its content is freed.

◆ rootTAGInput

TAGInput* rootTAGInput

Do not destroy rootTAGInput and currentTAGInput. They will be destroyed by other destroy functions. These are defined to fast access TAGInputs of the TAGGeneric linked list.
